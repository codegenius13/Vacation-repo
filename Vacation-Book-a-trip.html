This section of the vacation destination site requires some steps that you must follow in terms of booking and adding to your list of future trips.

Some informations have been provided for you in the table below on what this section is all about. The table below will help you to fill out what is required for the boxes below the page. Either you want to add to your list of trips or Book an upcoming trip.

Take a look at the instructions

Box 1: Names: In this section of the boxes to fill below, you will need to provide us with the correct names used to register for this website.
Box 2: Destination Information: In this section of the boxes to fill below, you will need to include the name of your chosen destination that you wish to visit from our available countries.
Box 3: Destination Location: This section of the boxes to fill below, you will need to provide us with the geographical location of the destination you have given.
Box 4: Destination Location URL: Thsi is requires you to give a description of the exact place you want to take your vacation. That is the room section you want to choose from the hotel being provided for this Vacation. Click here to see the details of the hotel rooms.
Box 5: Mode of transportation: In this section you are being asked to select your prefered mode of transportation. Select Airways for Transportation By Air while Seaways for Transportation By Water. The road transportation is for vacation at your closest vicinity.
Box 6: Hotel Name:In this section of the following boxes, you are asked to put in the name of the available hotels we have for this vacation destination project.
Box 7: Hotel Class:In this section of the boxes, you are asked a suitable class of hotel for your taste. These Hotel Classes vary in prices.
Hotel Classes:
  • High Class: Large room with a master bed. Air conditioner and heater are available
  • Medium Class: Medium size room with a medium size bed. Air conditioner and heater available
  • Lower Class: Small room size with a bed comfortable fot at most one person. No Air conditioner and heater available
  • Couples Class: Decorated to the taste of the couple. Also the same as the High class Hotel. Only Available On Honeymoon Vacation
Box 8: Code Number Input: Input your code number given to you while signing up for this site. Kindly sign up if there is no code number.
